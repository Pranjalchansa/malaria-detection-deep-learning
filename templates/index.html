About Malaria

Malaria is a life-threatening infectious disease caused by Plasmodium parasites transmitted through infected female Anopheles mosquitoes.

🦟 How It Spreads

The parasite enters the bloodstream through mosquito bites, travels to the liver, multiplies, and infects red blood cells.

⚠ Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Chills
  • Headache
  • Vomiting
  • Fatigue

🚨 Severe Effects

Untreated malaria can cause organ failure, anemia, brain damage, and even death.

🛡 Prevention

  • Mosquito nets
  • Repellents
  • Clean surroundings
