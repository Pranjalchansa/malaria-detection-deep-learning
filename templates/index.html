About Malaria

Malaria is a serious disease caused by Plasmodium parasites and transmitted through the bite of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes. Early detection is very important for proper treatment.

How It Spreads

  • Through mosquito bites
  • Infected blood transfusion (rare)
  • From mother to unborn baby

Prevention

  • Use mosquito nets while sleeping
  • Apply mosquito repellents
  • Keep water containers covered
  • Maintain clean surroundings
